# Product Guidelines: "Midnight Nebula" Design System

## Visual Direction & Aesthetic
- **Style:** Dark Mode / Futuristic Glassmorphism.
- **Keywords:** Premium, Fluid, Deep, Luminous, Precise.

## Color System
- **Background:** Deep Midnight (`#030014`) with subtle radial gradients.
- **Surface (Cards):** Semi-transparent `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.03)` with `10px` backdrop-blur.
- **Accents:** Electric Violet (`#8B5CF6`) and Cyan Sky (`#06B6D4`) used for "glow" effects.
- **Text:** Primary (White/90% opacity), Secondary (Slate-400).

## Typography
- **Headings:** Space Grotesk (Bold, wide tracking, gradient fills).
- **Body:** Inter (Clean, high readability, optimized line height).

## Section Concepts
### 1. The Navbar
- Floating Glass Dock, 70% width, 12px blur.

### 2. The Hero Section
- Impactful Typography + Ambient Motion.
- Spotlight effect follows cursor.
- "Status: Open for Internships" pill.

### 3. Project Cards
- Bento-style Glass Cards.
- Hover tilt effect.
- Embedded screenshot shadows.

### 4. Skills Section
- Border-beam animation on cards.

## Interaction & Motion
- Scroll Reveal (Fade up).
- Magnetic Buttons.
- Soft blur-in on page load.
